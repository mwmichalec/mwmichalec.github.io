ABOUT 3D

I have 3 years of practical experience with 3D modeling / texture work. All the models were hand-made by me, and presented images are renders, which are representing pre-baked textures with a (non reflective) material to represent their exact look in-game without calculating the reflections in real time. I am self-taught 3D artist, currently with main focus on game-engine optimized models. I design and craft both 3D models and textures, they are all tested on a "Second Life" platfom and are available there for sale. So far I have met great appreciation for my work and a massive positive feedback, which motivated me to further work.
